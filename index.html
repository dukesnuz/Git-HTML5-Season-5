What is this all about?

This is a very inspiring virtual self study group offered by Boston PHP. If you are not a member, join Boston PHP today. How do our incredible self study groups work? Each participant purchases the current book that we are using. For this season of HTML5 Brunch, Season 5 we are using Sams Teach Yourself HTML and CSS in 24 Hours by Julie Meloni. Each participant reads and completes the excercises according to the current Master Discussion List. Each participant then posts a link to their fantastic completed work in the corresponding message board thread. Questions and comments are also welcomed from participants. Participants can jump in and answer a fellow participant's question. The Senior Percolator(That would be me this season.) will scan your awesome work. Each week you will recieve an email updating progress of participants. There are no classroom meetings. Work from home or your favorite coffee shop. Your only cost is this terrific book. Self study groups are great fun and a really fabulous way to learn and improve your coding skills.

Upon successful completion of all work, your name will be chiseled onto the notable Self Study Wall of Accomplishments. This is a phenomenal accomplishment that you to can be a part of.

The Syllabus Top

  1. Your goal is to complete each "LISTING" for each hour
  2. You can use the exercises at the end of each chapter to create your own home page for this season.
  3. The "Try It Yourselfs" are not mandatory. These will be considered Xtra Stuff.
  4. Post a link to your completed work in the corresponding week's thread. View the Master Discussion List for this information.
